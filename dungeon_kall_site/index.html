About Dungeon.Kall

Hi! I'm Kall, a passionate Game Master with years of theatrical experience and a deep love for storytelling. I'm currently pursuing a Computer Science degree and dream of crafting immersive experiences for studios like Larian. When I'm not guiding heroes through dungeons, you'll find me building game prototypes and playing with new mechanics.

Theatre & Storytelling

My theatre background fuels every campaign I run. Over the years I've acted, directed and collaborated in countless productions. These experiences taught me how to weave suspense, humor and drama into my table‑top sessions. I bring this same energy to create unforgettable adventures for players.

Game Projects & Dev Demos

I tinker with game development whenever I can. On this site I'll post demos of my latest projects – from small prototypes exploring unique mechanics to full campaigns built with modern engines. Stay tuned for updates!

Goals & Aspirations

My ultimate goal is to work at top video game studios like Larian, blending my coding skills with my love for tabletop narratives. Through this blog I'll share progress on my journey, lessons learned and the challenges along the way.
