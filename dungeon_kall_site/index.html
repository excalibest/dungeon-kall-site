My Style & Approach

I blend character‑driven roleplay with exciting combat, tailoring every adventure to the players at the table. I love weaving your backstories into the plot and pivoting to dungeon crawls or tactical battles when that’s what you crave. Over the years I’ve run games for seasoned adventurers and curious newcomers alike – from regulars at my local game store to first‑timers looking to try something new.

Most of my sessions take place in home‑brew settings crafted just for your group, but I’m also happy to run official modules on request.

Games & Systems

I currently run Dungeons & Dragons 5E, D&D 2024, Pathfinder 2E Remastered, Electric Bastionland and Mythic Bastionland. I can quickly learn or relearn games like Blades in the Dark, Mouse Ritter and Panic at the Dojo, and I’m willing to explore new systems such as Starfinder, Call of Cthulhu, Fabula Ultima, Daggerheart or even the upcoming MCDM game and Cyberpunk on request.

Sessions & Pricing

A standard one‑shot or campaign session lasts about three hours. For small groups of up to four players the cost is $60 (that’s $20/hour). For larger groups of five to eight players, the rate is $15 per seat. If you’re interested in a recurring weekly, bi‑weekly or monthly campaign, please get in touch – pricing is flexible based on group size and frequency.

What’s included? I bring two cases of 250+ miniatures (monsters and characters), three LED lights to set the mood, two JBL speakers for atmospheric audio, and all the rulebooks you’ll need. I also provide pencils, dice and character sheets, so all you need to bring is your imagination!

Get in Touch

Ready to gather your party? Reach out to discuss your next campaign or one‑shot. I’m happy to answer questions and customize your experience. Sessions are very beginner‑friendly – for a year and a half I DMed weekly at a local game store for walk‑ins who were completely new to tabletop RPGs.
